FAQs

Frequently asked

What kind of brands do you work with?

We partner with ambitious businesses and founders ready to scale. Whether it’s an early-stage venture or an established brand seeking transformation, our focus is on companies with the vision and potential to lead in their space.

What is your average project price?

Our investments vary depending on the opportunity, but we typically engage with ventures that align with our strategic growth goals. We’re equally comfortable with minority growth investments and full acquisitions.

What is included in a branding project?

Beyond capital, we bring operational expertise, strategic support, and access to a global network. From brand development to scaling infrastructure, we work closely with founders to unlock long-term value.

Do I need to meet certain requirements to work with you?

We evaluate each opportunity on its own merits. Whether you’re a founder seeking growth capital or a brand ready for transformation, we look for ambition, strong fundamentals, and a clear path to scale.

Can you help reposition a struggling brand?

Yes. A core part of our strategy involves identifying underperforming businesses with strong fundamentals and transforming them into market leaders through brand revitalization and operational excellence.
